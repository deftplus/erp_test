Submitting an online report is very easy!

The software has the available service 1C-Report that enables sending reports to FTS, PFR, FSS, Rosstat, RusAlcoholRegulation directly from the software.

1C-Report also enables:
- maintaining correspondence with FTS, PFR, Rosstat;
- make revisions with FTS (the service "Information Taxpayer Service");
- making revisions with PFR (the service "Information Insurer Service");
- sending documents upon the request of FTS ;
- receiving various notifications from FTS;
-confirming the personified accounting information in a digital type that have been already passed to the PFR in hard copies ("retroconversion");
- generating reporting packages containing confirmation of submission to FTS to pass to credit institutions;
-checking correctness of reports online;
- sending declaration of production and turnover of alcohol manufactured products to the RusAlcoholRegulation;
-send reports generated by the third-party software to controlling authorities.

Users of1C:ITS PROF level may attach one business entity or individual entrepreneur to the service for free!
